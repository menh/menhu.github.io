RepPoints is proposed by Peking University, TsingHua and Microsoft, which is a better target detection method than boundary box.

Researchers from Peking University, Tsinghua University and Microsoft Asia Research Institute proposed a new and more refined
object representation method, RepPoints, which abandoned the popular boundary box representation, The results were as effective as the most
advanced anchor-based detection method. -xinzhiyuan

Target detection is one of the most basic tasks in computer vision. It is also a key component of many visual applications, including case segmentation, human pose analysis, visual reasoning and so on.

The purpose of target detection is to locate the target in the inage and provide the category of the target.

In recent years, with the rapid developement of deep neural network, the problem of target detection has also made considerable progress.

Current advanced target detectors rely heavily on rectangular boundaries to represent in different recognition, such as anchors, proposals and final prediction

Recently, Yang Ze, Wang LiWei and Shaohui Liu, from Peking University, Tsinghuaa University and Microsoft Asia Research Institute, have proposed a new and more elaborate object representation method,RepPoints(representative points), which is a set of sample points useful for positioning and recognition.

Given the taining ground truth to locate and recognize targets,RepPoints learns to arrange itself automatically in a way that limits the spatial range of target and represents semantically important local

In addition, RepPoints does not need to use anchor to sample the bounding box space.

The author demonstrates a Reppoint-based��anchor-free target dector,which can be implemented without mutiscale training and testing it.It is also as
effective as the most advanced anchor-based detectionmethod,and achieves 42.8 AP and 65.0 AP ramming on the COCO test-dev detection benchmark.

Discarding boundary boxes, a finer-grained target representation RepPoints

In the process of target detection, boundary box is the basic element of processing. The bundary box describes the target position in each stage of the target detector.

Althought the bounday frames are easy to calculate,they only provide tough position of the target,and do not fit the shape and attitude of the object.

Therfore,the feature extracted from the rule cell of the boundary box may be seriously affected by the background content containing a small amount of semantic information or the foreground area without information.

This may lead to a reduction in the quality of features,thus reducing the classification performance of target detection.

RepPoints training is driven by both target location and target recognition. Therfore, RepPoints is closely related to the ground-truth boundary box, and guides the detector to classify thr target correctly.
